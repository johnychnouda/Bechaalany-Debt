1. Introduction

Welcome to Bechaalany Connect ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

2. Free Application

Bechaalany Connect is a free application. We do not charge subscription fees, do not process in-app purchases, and do not collect any payment or billing information from our users. Access to the app is granted by an administrator at no cost.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Account Information

When you sign up for Bechaalany Connect, we collect:

  • Name: Your full name from your Google or Apple account
  • Email Address: Your email address for account creation and communication
  • Profile Photo: Your profile picture from your Google or Apple account (optional)

3.2 Business Data

To provide debt management functionality, we collect:

  • Customer Information: Names and contact details of your business customers
  • Debt Records: Information about customer debts, amounts, and payment status
  • Payment History: Records of customer debt payments (business revenue)
  • Product Catalog: Your business products, pricing, and inventory
  • Business Settings: Business name, currency preferences, and exchange rates

3.3 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • App Usage: Features used, screens viewed, and actions taken
  • Access Information: Trial status, access period, and expiration dates

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the debt management service
  • Manage your free trial and access status
  • Enable WhatsApp automation for payment reminders (if enabled)
  • Generate reports and receipts for your business
  • Backup and recover your business data
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Communicate important updates about the app

5. Payment Information Clarification

Important Distinction:

The app tracks customer debt payments (money your customers pay to your business), but we do NOT collect or process:

  • ❌ Your credit card information
  • ❌ Your bank account details
  • ❌ App subscription payments (the app is free)
  • ❌ In-app purchase transactions

All payment data in the app refers to your business operations (customer payments to you), not payments from you to us.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services:

  • Cloud Firestore: For real-time database storage
  • Firebase Authentication: For secure sign-in
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Control: Only you can access your business data

7. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal or business data with third parties. Your information is only accessible to:

  • You: The account owner
  • Administrator: Only for access management purposes
  • Firebase Services: For secure data storage (governed by Google's privacy policy)

8. WhatsApp Integration

If you enable WhatsApp automation:

  • The app opens WhatsApp with pre-filled messages
  • You control when and to whom messages are sent
  • We do not store WhatsApp message content
  • WhatsApp's privacy policy applies to messages sent

9. Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • Active Account: For as long as your account is active
  • Backups: Automatic backups are stored for data recovery
  • Deletion: You can delete your data anytime through the app settings
  • Account Closure: Data is permanently deleted upon account closure

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your stored data
  • Export: Download your data in PDF format
  • Delete: Remove specific records or clear all data
  • Recover: Restore data from backups
  • Opt-out: Disable WhatsApp automation anytime

11. Children's Privacy

Bechaalany Connect is intended for business use by adults. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

12. Third-Party Services

The app uses these third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and data storage
  • Google Sign-In: Account creation (optional)
  • Sign in with Apple: Account creation (optional)
  • WhatsApp: Payment reminder automation (optional)

These services have their own privacy policies governing their data practices.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new privacy policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Sending a notification for significant changes
